About School of South Asia

School of South Asia is an independent co-educational day school in Lahore, Pakistan. It offers a well-balanced international curriculum, which recognizes the value of and integrates the Pakistani national curriculum. School of South Asia has a highly qualified and experienced faculty. The purpose-built campus spreads over six acres; with a capacity of almost one thousand students. The campus is fully equipped with necessary resources learning resources.

School of South Asia carries the privilege to enjoy partnerships with international educational institutions, along with faculty training sessions and student placement in various high ranked, well-reputed Universities in Pakistan and across the world. It is affiliated with Cambridge Assessment and International Education (CAIE), Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education Lahore, and membership with Advance Ed is in process.

School of South Asia strongly believes in developing lifelong learning skills amongst its students and provides numerous opportunities for students to enroll themselves in after school clubs and societies, community outreach programs, career counseling & guidance programs. The diverse and inclusive environment at PAS enables the students to develop and excel in mental, physical, social & moral endeavors and make them collaborate successfully with people from various backgrounds and nationalities.
